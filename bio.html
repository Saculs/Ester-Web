Ester Parasková (* 1993) is currently a student at the Intermedial Confrontation studio led by Michal Pěchouček and Dominik Gajarský at the Academy of Arts, Architecture and Design in Prague. In her work, the author works with raw sincerity, which she projects into her works through expressive gesticulation and often creates controlled chaos on canvases. The structure of her paintings is constantly evolving - she uses layering of colors, she experiments with materials, but also with the canvas itself. Parasková thus creates situations in which she directly plays with the objectivity of the medium of painting.
